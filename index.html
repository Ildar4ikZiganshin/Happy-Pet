Our Additional Services

Pet Washing

Pet Washing

Keep your furry friend looking and smelling their best with our professional pet bathing service.

Our gentle, pet-friendly products and brushing techniques will leave your pet's coat clean, soft, and shiny. We carefully clean those hard-to-reach areas while making the experience stress-free for your pet.

Nail Trimming

Nail Trimming

Maintain your pet's healthy feet and avoid painful overgrown nails with our expert nail trimming service.

Our professionals use proper tools and techniques to safely trim nails to the optimal length for your pet's comfort. Properly trimmed nails prevent scratching, snagging, and injuries.

Haircuts

Haircuts

From breed-standard cuts to customized trims, our expert pet stylists will give your furry pal a fresh, polished look.

We carefully shape and thin coats using professional grooming shears and clippers for a healthy, even cut tailored to your pet's needs. Haircuts increase comfort and make home brushing easier between grooms.
